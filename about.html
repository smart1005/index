About Thebes Mart

Rooted in community. Built to serve everyone.

Our Story

Thebes Mart started with a simple dream: to bring everything a household, school, or small business could ever need into one friendly, affordable store right in the heart of the community.

From humble beginnings as a small corner shop, we've grown into a full-fledged superstore stocking thousands of products — from your morning toiletries to school bags for the kids, from rice and cooking oil to light bulbs and phone cables.

We believe that convenience shouldn't come at a premium. Every price on our shelf reflects our commitment to making quality goods accessible to everyone.
